Definition 2.4.3

Let \(\| \cdot \|_\mu: \C^m \rightarrow \R \) and \(\| \cdot \|_\nu: \C^n \rightarrow \R \) be vector norms. Define \(\| \cdot \|_{\mu,\nu} : \C^{m \times n} \rightarrow \R \) by

\begin{equation*} \| A \|_{\mu,\nu} = \sup_{ \begin{array}{c} x \in \C^n \\ x \neq 0 \end{array} } \frac{\| A x \|_\mu}{\| x \|_{\nu}}. \end{equation*}
