Definition 2.3.11

Given \(p \geq 1 \text{,}\) the vector \(p\)-norm \(\| \cdot \|_p : \C^n \rightarrow \R \) is defined for \(x \in \C^n \) by

\begin{equation*} \| x \|_p = \sqrt[p]{\vert \chi_0 \vert^p + \cdots + \vert \chi_{n-1} \vert^p} = \left( \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} \vert \chi_i \vert^p \right)^{1/p}. \end{equation*}
