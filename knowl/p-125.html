We also want to show that \(\| A \|_\infty \geq \max_i \| \widehat a_i \|_1\text{.}\) Let \(k \) be such that \(\max_i \| \widehat a_i \|_1 = \| \widehat a_k\|_1 \) and pick \(y = \left( \begin{array}{c} \psi_0 \\ \psi_1 \\ \vdots \\ \psi_{n-1} \end{array} \right) \) so that \(\widehat a_k^T y = \vert \alpha_{k,0} \vert + \vert \alpha_{k,1} \vert + \cdots + \vert \alpha_{k,n-1} \vert = \| \widehat a_k \|_1 \text{.}\) (This is a matter of picking \(\psi_i \) so that \(\vert \psi_i \vert =1\) and \(\psi_i \alpha_{k,i} = \vert \alpha_{k,i} \vert \text{.}\)) Then

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{rcl} \| A \|_\infty = \max_{\|x\|_1=1} \| A x\|_\infty \amp=\amp \max_{\|x\|_1=1} \left\| \left( \begin{array}{c} \widehat a_0^T \\ \hline \widehat a_1^T \\ \hline \vdots \\ \hline \widehat a_{m-1}^T \end{array} \right) x \right\|_\infty \geq \left\| \left( \begin{array}{c} \widehat a_0^T \\ \hline \widehat a_1^T \\ \hline \vdots \\ \hline \widehat a_{m-1}^T \end{array} \right) y \right\|_\infty \\ \amp = \amp \left\| \left( \begin{array}{c} \widehat a_0^T y\\ \hline \widehat a_1^T y\\ \hline \vdots \\ \hline \widehat a_{m-1}^T y \end{array} \right) \right\|_\infty \geq \vert \widehat a_k^T y \vert = \widehat a_k^T y =\| \widehat a_k \|_1. = \max_i \| \widehat a_i \|_1 \end{array} \end{equation*}
