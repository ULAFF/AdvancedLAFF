Theorem 2.4.15 Equivalence of matrix norms

Let \(\| \cdot \|: \C^{m \times n} \rightarrow \R \) and \(\vert \vert \vert \cdot \vert \vert \vert: \C^{m \times n} \rightarrow \R \) both be matrix norms. Then there exist positive scalars \(\sigma \) and \(\tau \) such that for all \(A \in \C^{m \times n} \)

\begin{equation*} \sigma \| A \| \leq \vert \vert \vert A \vert \vert \vert \leq \tau \| A \|. \end{equation*}
