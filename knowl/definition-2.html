Definition 2.3.4

The vector 2-norm \(\| \cdot \|_2 : \C^n \rightarrow \R \) is defined for \(x \in \C^n \) by

\begin{equation*} \| x \|_2 = \sqrt{ \vert \chi_0 \vert^2 + \cdots + \vert \chi_{n-1} \vert^2 } . = \sqrt{x^H x} = \sqrt{ \overline \chi_0 \chi_0 + \cdots + \overline \chi_{n-1} \chi_{n-1} } \end{equation*}
