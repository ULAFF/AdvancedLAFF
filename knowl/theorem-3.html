Theorem 2.3.14 Equivalence of vector norms

Let \(\| \cdot \|: \C^n \rightarrow \R \) and \(\vert \vert \vert \cdot \vert \vert \vert: \C^n \rightarrow \R \) both be vector norms. Then there exist positive scalars \(\sigma \) and \(\tau \) such that for all \(x \in \Cn \)

\begin{equation*} \sigma \| x \| \leq \vert \vert \vert x \vert \vert \vert \leq \tau \| x \|. \end{equation*}
