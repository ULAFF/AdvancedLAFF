\(\| \alpha x \|_\infty = \vert \alpha \vert \| x \|_\infty \) (\(\| \cdot \|_\infty \) is homogeneous):

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{lcl} \| \alpha x \|_\infty \amp =\amp \max_{i=0}^{n-1} \vert \alpha \chi_i \vert \\ \amp =\amp \max_{i=0}^{n-1} \vert \alpha \vert \vert \chi_i \vert \\ \amp =\amp \vert \alpha \vert \max_{i=0}^{n-1} \vert \chi_i \vert \\ \amp =\amp \vert \alpha \vert \| x \|_\infty. \end{array} \end{equation*}
