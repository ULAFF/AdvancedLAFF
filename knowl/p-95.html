Let us start by interpreting this. How "big" \(A \) is, as measured by \(\| A \|_{\mu,\nu} \text{,}\) is defined as the most that \(A \) magnifies the length of nonzero vectors, where the length of a vector (\(x\)) is measured with norm \(\| \cdot \|_\nu \) and the length of a transformed vector (\(A x \)) is measured with norm \(\| \cdot \|_\mu \text{.}\)
