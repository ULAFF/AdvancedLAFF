Let \(A \in \C^{m \times n} \) and partition \(A = \left( \begin{array}{c} \widehat a_0^T \\ \hline \widehat a_1^T \\ \hline \vdots \\ \hline \widehat a_{m-1}^T \end{array} \right) \text{.}\) Show that

\begin{equation*} \| A \|_\infty = \max_{0 \leq i \lt m} \| \widehat a_i \|_1 = \max_{0 \leq i \lt m} \left( \vert \alpha_{i,0} \vert + \vert \alpha_{i,1} \vert + \cdots + \vert \alpha_{i,n-1} \vert \right) \end{equation*}

Notice that in this exercise \(\widehat a_i \) is really \(( \widehat a_i^T )^T \) since \(\widehat a_i^T \) is the label for the \(i \)th row of matrix \(A \text{.}\)
